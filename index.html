Protocol Knowledge Base

How do you handle Freeze Protection?

We do not use duct tape or zip ties. Leveraging Northern industrial standards, we install sealed, thermal-grade insulation (Armaflex) that withstands hard freezes. This isn't just wrapping; it is thermal shielding to protect your PVC and valves.

Do you offer Green-to-Clean services?

Yes. We specialize in transforming swamp-like conditions into potable-standard clarity. Our "Green-to-Clean" process involves heavy debris removal, aggressive chemical shock, and often a filter media change to completely reset your water quality baseline.

Is there a long-term service contract?

No. We operate on a month-to-month performance basis. We believe retention should be earned through water quality, not binding paperwork.

Do you service Salt Water Systems?

Yes. Salt cells require specific LSI calibration to prevent calcium scaling on the plates. We monitor salinity levels and cell output as part of the standard protocol.
